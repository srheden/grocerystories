Grocery Stories

Hello. I’m Sarah Eden. Grocery Stories is about community and food. It started out mostly about grocery stores — places I’ve been interested ever since shopping at and then working in a family-run Lebanese grocery store as a teenager — but then it grew because getting the ingredients is only the middle.

You’ll find stories here about the people, stores, cafés, and restaurants I love most, in London and in places I visit. You will find recipes worth recreating. You’ll also find information about food-related events I’m interested in, and maybe you’ll be interested in them too.

If you’ve got a place or event you think belongs on these pages, please let me know using the form below. I’d love to hear from you.
